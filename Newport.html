about the project

Location

The Newport multifunctional complex is being built in the center of a prestigious part of Tashkent and at the intersection of four roads: Nukus, Babur, Kichik Beshagach streets and the Small Ring Road. The project has a convenient location, good transport links and important urban social facilities nearby: parks, schools, kindergartens, clinics, hotels, business centers, airport. Islam Karimov.

Safety

All Newport buildings are equipped with the best security and safety systems, they are earthquake-resistant and have smart home functionality. There is 24-hour video surveillance around the perimeter of the complex, in lobby areas and cozy entrances, and there are also concierges and security guards.

Service

A management company with international expertise was involved in providing services; NewportSupport mobile service application is being developed. Additional services for residents and guests: cleaning, dog walking, calling a taxi, meeting and seeing off at the airport and train station.

Concept

The Newport multifunctional residential complex in Tashkent is a unique project with a mixed-use concept brought to life. This New Urbanism concept is an alternative to new developments that lead to car dependency. A balanced combination of apartments, commercial real estate, service and entertainment areas also creates a safe urban space. We are creating an exemplary model of what city life can be.

Architecture

Newport Art Nouveau architecture. This innovative direction in art arose against the background of the rise of industry, the active development of science and technology, and we did not choose it by chance. Modernism is a turning point in content, the embodiment of a new era and technology, just like Newport! Architectural and design solutions of the residential complex - a symbiosis of brevity and elegant geometry
